How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

At https://hz.mynetpay.nl/Login you can log in with your HZ account and upload a file that you want to print. Using your HZ pass by any HZ printer you will be able to print them.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

At a HZ printer you can scan your pass and choose "scan" and the files will be sent to your mail inbox.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the reception at the bottom floor and ask the receptionist.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Activate your HZ pass at the first floor and when you want to park you can scan your pass at the gate.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Go to the site and book a project space.
